  • Editorial note: Whitman probably inscribed the material in this notebook in the early 1850s as he was composing the first (1855) edition of Leaves of Grass. Content from leaf 10 verso was revised and used in the poem eventually titled "The Sleepers," which first appeared as the fourth poem in that edition. The same passage likely also relates to lines in the 1855 poem eventually titled "Song of Myself." Content from leaf 13 recto may relate to other sections of the poem eventually titled "Song of Myself."
